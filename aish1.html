Women's day.

Dedicated to my dainty women, my mother and my sister.

She fought alone, to give her family a good life...

She fought with all the bruises still raw...

She fought through all the pain wrenched deep in her heart...

She cried all night, and woke up in the morning with a smile on her face and a new hope of a new dawn...

She spiked every tear she shed into a new spine...

She blossomed into something so superior and so out of the world...

She did not choose a path, but created one on her own...

She is bold...

She is strong...

She is unfathomable...

She is a mother...

She is a daughter...

She is a sister...

She is a wife...

She is a true woman.

Women's day... It may seem like just any ordinary day until you know the importance of being a woman and the women in your life...Today is the celebration of all those brave women who fought through the odds and social stigmas of the society and made their way to achieve everything they want in life. This is for every woman who doesn't give up hope and proves to the world that a woman is indeed unstoppable when she learns what she deserves.

A very happy women's day to all the woman who know what they want and make their own crown!

By,

Aishwarya Rao Chelikani
